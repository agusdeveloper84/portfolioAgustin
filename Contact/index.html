Since I was a child I have been experimenting with design and trying to create websites. I remember playing around with Adobe Dreamweaver in the 90's and being fascinated with this.

Growing up I discovered that this was my passion and that I wanted this for my life. I found this career not only to be my passion but also a hobby, that is why I spend my free time creating and coding, enjoying every minute working on it, to improve myself day by day.

Through the years I have been studying at Colleges, Boot Camps and online courses to improve my knowledge and become better than never. I am really passionate about this career, good at problem solving, love to create and help. Enjoying everyday coding is my key to success.

I believe that the skills I have gained as a Front-End Web Developer in the past year will contribute to this growing industry. I have a strong desire to work in a team that will help create optimized and creative websites that showcase amazing brands and products. I believe that this career is a key to help people to build and make their ideas come true.
